Title: Login bug regression sample

As a returning user,
I want to sign in with my registered email and password,
so that I can access my account securely.

Acceptance criteria:
- valid email: test@demo.com
- valid password: Password123
- Success is indicated by the message: "Login successful. Welcome back."
- Failure is indicated by the message: "Invalid email or password."
- A login attempt with the correct email and a wrong password must fail.
- A login attempt with the correct email and the correct password must succeed.
